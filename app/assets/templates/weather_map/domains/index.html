This category includes artifacts associated with documents that generated network traffic. While it can often be inferred that the callback location is either a C2 channel or a site for additional malware downloads, this indicator alone does not provide that level of insight.

Malware & BotnetBotnet ControllersThese machines have been identified as being the controller to an infected victim. Although an attacker could still use several abstraction layers to mask they actions to these machines, it does provide one step closer to the origination of the attack.
